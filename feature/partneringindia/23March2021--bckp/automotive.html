Automotive Equipment

Mitsubishi Electric offers a range of automotive equipment technologies that today forms the core of several general, hybrid as well as next-generation clean energy vehicles offered by the top automobile OEMs in the world.

Charging & Starting Products

Mitsubishi Electric offers compact and lightweight alternators and starters to help meet the growing demand for high-performance electrical engine components that can help improve fuel efficiency and comfort.

Engine Management Products

As an automotive equipment manufacturer, Mitsubishi Electric harnesses its experience and advanced technologies to offer a broad range of engine management products that help deliver superior engine performance.

Electric Power Steering System Products

Electric power steering significantly minimizes engine power loss as compared to hydraulic power steering. It also offers a more comfortable driving experience with enhanced stability and responsiveness, which is difficult to achieve with the hydraulic power steering system.

Car Multimedia Products
