Mitsubishi Electric Air Conditioners – Creating a Comfortable Environment

With summer around the corner it is essential to keep the indoor temperature cool and comfortable. One has to make an essential choice for an air conditioning solution matching the modern life comfort without impacting electricity usage. As per the study carried out by ‘The Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE)’, it recommends that an air-conditioner’s default setting at 24-degree temperature will help save a lot of Energy. The rational behind is that normal human body temperature is approximately 36 to 37 degree Celsius and maintaining a temperature around 18-21 degrees could lead to wastage of Energy as it may not maintain the best ambient temperature for the body. The practice had already been adopted in countries like Japan and maintained in various places through the regulation.

Mitsubishi Electric has been a prominent player in air conditioner technology in Japan, following the government mandate, it offers a wide range of cooling solution without jeopardising the environment, to maintain the perfect balance of sustainability. Mitsubishi Electric promises to deliver long lasting cooling solutions through their durability in the design together with advanced technologies. Ensuring this also is its own manufactured key parts such as compressor, motor and semiconductor which enable an effective and quiet cooling experience.

Our Concepts - Dedication towards Excellence

The growing sectors in India such as Healthcare, residential and other commercial buildings have shown consistent demand for improved technologies and reliable solutions year-around. Providing a wide range of advantages over conventional ACs, Mitsubishi Electric inverters are designed in a way that provide efficient and customised air conditioning solutions packed with advanced features that make them our customer’s first choice to meet the requirements of different seasons.

Powerful Cooling

The automatic one-touch powerful cool system ensures faster cooling through Mitsubishi Electric Air conditioners. It produces 10% more air flow than the “high speed” fan, cooling the room in less than 15 minutes, then automatically returning to the regular setting.

Greater Comfort and Smart Savings

The Econo Cool is a one touch operation that automatically adjusts the direction of the airflow based on the temperature at the air outlet. The set temperature can therefore be 2 degrees Celsius above conventional temperature settings without loss of comfort with a 20% increase in energy efficiency. Hence, we ensure greater comfort and smart savings on bills.

High-Power Compressor & Enhanced Motor Efficiency

The compressor is the heart of the air conditioner. We have a proprietary technology that can achieve stable and long-term performance. The other technology through proprietary built in “Poki-Poki Motor" dramatically enhances the performance, as it has more wires around the coil making it more efficient compared to conventional motor.

Safeguarding AC with Dual Barrier Coating

Dual Barrier Coating is an advanced technology that protects air conditioners from the ill effects of dirt and dust. This technology helps in reducing the attachment of dust and oil mist that coats the inside of the conditioners. The coating material greatly reduces the attachment of dust which is mixed in the air. This lends the units the power to work at full performance. The reduction in the dirt also means the bad smell and odours go away. With less dirt, the necessity of cleaning the air conditioner also lessens.

Another upside of the dual barrier coating technology is its effect on the planet. The technology is environment-friendly and does not cause harm to the ozone. Currently, the air conditioners which are equipped with dual barrier coating technology are effective to protect the unit from the corrosion and erosion and fill the room with cool, clean, and fresh air.

Transcending from Conventional filters

The cutting-edge technology is one of the latest additions to the new age of air conditioning. Mitsubishi Electric air-conditioners also provide a range of standard- Anti Mold Filter as well as optional Electrostatic Anti – Allergy Enzyme filter, Microparticle Catching Filter, High Density filter that can remove 95% of airborne particulate (2.0µm or more), available in many premium product ranges. The equipped Plasma Filter has the potential to filter and purify the air of bacteria and viruses from indoor air through filtration. The combination of Plasma filter with branch box increases the air-conditioner’s productivity by leveraging its performance and application in day to day operation. Additionally, the designed anti- corrosion features available in room air-conditioner series help to withstand a high corrosive environment ensuring its durability in the long run despite all odds.
