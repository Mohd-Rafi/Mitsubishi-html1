Semiconductors & Devices

Mitsubishi Electric provides a wide range of semiconductors and devices. These include high-power devices and power modules that enable highly efficient power control, optical devices that deliver optimum performance for fibre-optic communication devices, high frequency devices ranging from satellite communications to radios and mobile phones, and an array of modules that help improve information interface.

Power Modules

Manufactured using state-of-the-art technologies, power modules by Mitsubishi Electric offer the highest levels of reliability and performance along with impressive energy savings for the systems they are used in.

High-Power Devices

High-power devices, including high voltage rectifier diodes, are designed to support high-current and high-voltage environment of rollers used in electric power systems, subways, and steel works, thereby contributing to increased efficiency as well as energy savings.

Driver IC, Sensors

The extensive line-up of power semiconductors by Mitsubishi Electric includes HVICs (High-Voltage IC), transistor arrays, and sensors. High voltage ICs directly drive IGBTs or power MOSFETs and transistor arrays can condense multiple transistors on one chip. Acceleration sensors and pressure sensors, on the other hand, are instrumental to the miniaturization and energy savings of such devices.

Optical Devices

At the forefront of the industry, Mitsubishi Electric is a leading developer and producer of laser diodes and modules. This range includes laser diodes for industrial applications and display, and the latest optical fibre communications that are used for broadband and telecommunications networks.

High-Frequency Devices

Supporting the advancement of wireless communication networks, Mitsubishi Electric has been involved in the development of high frequency devices. These products are manufactured with the latest electronics and technologies and are combined with new high-performance materials.
