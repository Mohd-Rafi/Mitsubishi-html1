Jet Towels Vs. Paper Towels

In today’s world, people are always on the lookout for something superior, something that improves upon the original. Over time, customers have come to witness a number of significant innovations that make everyday life better. One such product is Jet Towels. Before this, wiping your hands meant using a towel or a tissue. But with the advent of Jet Towels, the way we dry our hands after using the restroom has changed for good.

As the name suggests, a Jet Towel is a hand dryer that uses jet blasts of air on a person’s hand to dry them off. Owing to the ease provided by this machine, you can find them everywhere these days. In fact, they are so extensively used that you can spot them in shopping complexes, restaurants, airports, corporate setups and other places as well.

Now comes the interesting debate. On one hand, you have this wonderful machine that provides so much convenience and on the other, you have the paper towels. While there are some who support paper towels, there are many who have transitioned to using jet towels.

Since the tussle about which one is better is a never-ending one, here are some important aspects to put things into perspective: -

1. Environmental friendly

We all are well aware of the fact that paper is produced from trees and when we use more and more paper towels, it burdens the environment even more. Add to that, the paper towels that we use is non-biodegradable waste that harms the environment when burnt; they produce a lot of greenhouse gases.

On the other hand, Jet Towels produced by Mitsubishi Electric are a much better choice as they are quite environment-friendly. When compared to paper towels, they emit lesser greenhouse gases. Additionally, there’s no waste generated. So, keeping the environmental aspect in mind, using a Jet Towel is a better option.

2. Cost-Effectiveness

If you look at it from a long-term standpoint, Jet Towels are the more cost-effective option because the person has to just pay for electricity consumption. At Mitsubishi Electric, we use energy-efficient technology that saves your power bills which directly reduces the running cost. Additionally, the high-speed Jet Towels have a faster drying speed and are more efficient in terms of power consumption. With all these advantages, Jet Towels prove to be the more viable option when compared to traditional Paper Towels.

3. They Are Hygienic

Another big benefit of using Jet Towels over paper towels is that they are hygienic. When you use a paper towel from the same roll in toilets and urinals, there is a high risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, a Jet Towel benefits from a completely touch-free operation which reduces this risk. So, if your hygiene is your priority in public places, then using the Jet Towel is the best option.

4. No Carbon Footprint

Paper Towels lead to an increase in carbon footprint because they require recurring logistics cost while re-stocking. Furthermore, they also pose a problem at the time of disposal. According to statistics, paper towels, on average, are responsible for about 56 g of gas emissions whereas their counterparts are way better with the emissions between 9g and 40g.

5. Washroom management

When you opt for Jet Towels: -

  • You don’t have to worry about the dispensers of paper.
  • You will never run out of paper.
  • You will not have to worry about reordering or storing the towels.
  • You don’t have to empty the dustbins.
  • You don’t have to worry about overflowing water receptacles.
