Package Air Conditioners: A Functional and Luxurious Add-on to Your Space

traditional air conditioner comprises both indoor and outdoor components. The outdoor components help the refrigerant to gain pressure and dissipate heat. In a cyclic operation, the indoor components carry out the absorption of heat from the indoor air and return cool air back into the house. But, when it comes to package air conditioners, both these components are present in a single outdoor packaged unit.

Mitsubishi Electric has pioneered and built package air conditioners that consume less power and have low overall running costs. Its range of package air conditioners includes Inverter Series Mr. Slim R410A and Mr. Slim R410A Non Inverter.

Upon further segmentation, the product line-up of the Inverter Series Mr. Slim R410A comprise of Ceiling-concealed and Ceiling-cassette. On the other hand, when we talk about the Mr. Slim R410A Non Inverter product line-up, it comprises of Ceiling concealed (PE-P Series) and 4-way Ceiling-cassette (R410A Non-Inverter PLP Series). All these products are known for offering enhanced performance and energy savings.

Let’s consider Cassette ACs in particular. They are known to provide several advantages along with adding a luxurious edge to any space. But, you may wonder how can an AC possibly do that? The answer is simple. Imagine a cassette AC installed within a false-ceiling which is known to enhance the décor of any room, adding a touch of opulence to that space. The cassette AC comes in to further augment this appeal.

Sleek, Elegant, and Luxurious

One of the effective advantages of a cassette AC is the fact that it can be set-up and tucked within a false ceiling. Its powerful cooling and more or less central placement in the roof makes it ideal for huge living-cum-dining areas where wall-mounted split ACs are less effective. Along with providing ample cooling, a cassette AC is known for the sleekness and elegance it adds to a particular space. It effortlessly enhances the aesthetics of any room as only the thin fascia panel is visible to the room’s occupants.

Along with offering a range of package air conditioners, Mitsubishi Electric also offers different room air conditioners and VRF systems – City Multi. One of the parameters on which the package air conditioners by Mitsubishi Electric distinguish themselves is the aesthetic quotient. The brand’s package air conditioners add a significant luxurious appeal to spaces they are installed in.

Making A Strong Case for Package Air Conditioners

A package air conditioner is a lot more than just a luxurious add-on to your space; it offers a host of advantages as well. Take a look: -

  • Saves Space: A great thing about using package air conditioners such as a cassette AC is that it does not take up space on the windows or walls. It is ceiling mounted.
  • Easy maintenance and repair: In the case of package air conditioners, the technician has to check only a single cabinet. Hence, these types of air conditioners are very easy to maintain.
  • Provides cool and warm air to large spaces: Package air conditioners can be used for providing cool or warm air conditioning to large spaces depending on the requirement.
  • Installation: The installation of a package air conditioner is a lot more cost-effective because it is only a single unit and does not require much time in its set-up.
  • Versatility: Package air conditioning units are versatile in nature. They can be used in homes and different commercial establishments. They also find extensive applications in restaurants, small halls, telephone exchanges, and other such spaces.

Versatile, aesthetic and effective – package air conditioners indeed have a lot going for them.
