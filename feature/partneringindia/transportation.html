Transportation Systems

Contributing to safe, reliable, and comfortable railway transportation, Mitsubishi Electric offers a range of innovative solutions that include state-of-the-art products and systems as well as top class networks for maintenance and service. Mitsubishi Electric has a clear focus on building eco-conscious products and manufacturing processes. At the same time, we are constantly innovating to deliver energy-saving electronics technologies, systems and components which are used for various types of railway rolling stock.

Traction Motor

The lightweight and state-of-the-art AC traction motor by Mitsubishi Electric has an effective self-ventilated cooling system along with high capacity. These railway traction systems expertly adapt to Indian environmental conditions to ensure reliable and stable running of rolling stock.

Converter-Inverter & VVVF System

This system offers a lightweight, advanced, and compact design with stable controllability. Additionally, these controllers are designed to provide high performance and easy maintenance.

Auxiliary Power Supply System

The SiC-based Auxiliary Power Supply system by Mitsubishi Electric is the latest in compact and lightweight auxiliary power units. It helps regulate the power output to the rolling stock’s auxiliary loads. By utilising a Silicon Carbide-based semiconductor, it also helps achieve higher energy efficiency.

Train Control and Management System (TCMS)

Known as the “Brain of the Train”, TCMS is an intelligent transport system which helps monitor the sub-system functions of rolling stock, provide the run-time status information to the train operator, and manage wayside communications. TCMS technology by Mitsubishi Electric is an integral part of many metro rail networks in India.
